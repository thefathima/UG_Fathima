About What We Do & Who We Are

About Us

Welcome to QuickFix – Gizmo Solutions, the innovative platform dedicated to extending the life of your home appliances while championing environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

At QuickFix, our mission is simple: Empower homeowners to take control of their appliance maintenance, encourage the reuse of functional equipment, and significantly reduce electronic waste. We aim to 'Revive Appliances One Home at a Time', transforming how you approach appliance longevity.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no appliance is discarded prematurely, and every homeowner is equipped with the knowledge and tools to repair and maintain their gadgets. QuickFix seeks to become the go-to resource for appliance care, sparing our landfills and contributing to a cleaner planet.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: We believe in the power of repair over replacement, minimizing waste and preserving our environment.
  • Empowerment: By providing easy-to-follow repair guides and spare parts, we enable you to fix common appliance issues confidently.
  • Innovation: We constantly update our offerings and platform features to stay at the forefront of the home maintenance industry.
  • Community: QuickFix is more than a service—it's a community of like-minded individuals sharing advice and support.

Our Story

Founded by a team passionate about technology and the environment, QuickFix began as an idea to address the staggering rates of e-waste. We noticed that many appliances are discarded not due to lack of functionality, but due to a lack of accessible repair options. QuickFix was born to bridge this gap.

Our Team

Our diverse team comprises experts in IT, sustainability, and customer service, all committed to providing you with a seamless and informative experience. We're always here to help, guide, and inspire your next appliance repair project.

Join Us

Join the QuickFix family and take a step towards sustainable living. Whether you're a seasoned DIYer or new to home repairs, QuickFix provides you with the resources to keep your appliances running smoothly. Let's reduce e-waste together, one repair at a time.
